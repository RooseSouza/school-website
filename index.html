About the Saunstha

Gopalkrishna Vidhyaprasarak Saunstha was established in 2006 with a noble vision to provide quality education to local communities. Founded under the guidance of Late Shri Sitaram Shrikant alias Anant Kanekar, the institution began as a small nursery and steadily grew into a respected educational organization.

  • 2006: Started with “Shishuvatika” (Nursery)
  • 2011: Official registration as a Saunstha
  • 2012–2014: Launch of Primary section (Marathi Medium)
  • Present: 180+ students across sections

Our mission is holistic development through academics, values, and practical life skills. Students graduating from our institution continue to excel, proving the strength of the foundation we provide.
